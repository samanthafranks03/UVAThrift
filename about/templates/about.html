Our Mission

UVA Thrift connects UVA students through sustainable, no-cost item exchange. Our goal is to reduce waste, make essential items accessible, and support a community of generosity on Grounds.

Why Second-Hand Shopping Matters

Environmental Impact

Choosing second-hand keeps usable items out of landfills and lowers the demand for new production, conserving energy, water, and materials.

Save Money

College expenses add up fast. Free item exchange helps students access what they need without financial strain.

Build Community

UVA Thrift encourages students to help one another, share resources, and strengthen a culture of sustainability and support across Grounds.

Our Policies

Eligibility

• This platform is intended to be used by current UVA students.
• Users are responsible for keeping their account information secure.

Listing Guidelines

• All items must be offered completely free.
• Items must be accurately described and in usable condition.
• Photos must show the actual item.
• Misleading, inappropriate, or unsafe listings are not allowed.

Prohibited Items

Users may NOT give away:
• Weapons
• Alcohol and drugs
• Hazardous items
• Stolen items
• Academic assignment materials (tests, homework solutions, etc.)
• Anything banned by UVA or state/federal law
• Any additional items deemed inappropraite by the UVA Thrift team

Item Quality & Responsibility

• Items should be clean and functional.
• Any defects must be clearly stated.
• Users accept items at their own risk.

Communication & Pickup Safety

• All communication must be respectful.
• Meetups should occur only in public UVA locations.
• UVA Thrift does not handle shipping—exchanges are in person only.

No Payments Allowed

• No user may ask for compensation of any kind.
• Attempting to charge for an item is a violation and may result in suspension.

Fairness

• Items should be given to the first confirmed requester unless stated otherwise.
• Items may not be “held” in exchange for payment.

Flagging Listings

• Users can flag any listing that appears unsafe, inappropriate, misleading, or in violation of our policies.
• Flagged listings are reviewed by the UVA Thrift team.
• If necessary, listings will be removed and user accounts may be warned or suspended.
• Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.

Liability

UVA Thrift is a peer-to-peer, free-exchange platform. We are not responsible for item condition, disputes between users, or any issues arising during meetups.
