Our Mission

UVA Thrift connects UVA students through sustainable commerce, making it easy to buy and sell pre-loved items while reducing waste and supporting our community.

Why Second-Hand Shopping Matters

Environmental Impact

Buying second-hand reduces waste in landfills and decreases the demand for new production, which saves energy, water, and raw materials.

Save Money

College life is expensive. Second-hand shopping helps students afford quality items at a fraction of the original cost, making student budgets stretch further.

Build Community

Connect with fellow UVA students, share resources, and foster a culture of sustainability and mutual support on Grounds.
